Responsiveness

Desktop

For screen widths above 1024px, the layout should remain consistent and visually balanced. Page content should be centered and maintain appropriate spacing to ensure a clean, readable appearance.

Mobile

The design must remain functional and visually clear on mobile devices, with a minimum supported screen width of 320px. This includes readable text, accessible buttons, and content that adapts without breaking.
